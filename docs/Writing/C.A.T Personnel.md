Employees of [[C.A.T]]


All branches of C.A.T follow a strict uniform policy, while looking stylish they also each distinctly class their occupation using standout colours and symbols, the extremists are no exception, making their own uniforms to fit their needs.

Green Personnel are the scientists and lead researchers. Nicknamed Scrubs by the civilian populace due to their attire resembling surgeon gear.

Yellow Personnel are security and mechanical engineers, designated as both due to the convenience of pressure operated exoskeletons in both fields. Their long snaking breathing apparatus’s to protect the suits operators from toxic fumes and similar hazards gave them the moniker ‘Trunks’
