The gun.

Created by [[C.A.T]] as an experiment into making soldiers fall in love with war. Connects to users' brains through the mask.

Each individual gun is tied to a particular mask, and getting further away from the gun by more than a few metres causes the user to feel an increasing compulsion to go back and get it again.

If you're kept away from your gun for too long you go mad and mindlessly search for it, or even try to take other guns.

The more you use your gun the more you get tied to it and the worse the compulsion to have it gets. You become more reliant on it for everything including combat, making decisions, socialising and communicating, or doing anything really.

The gun has the ability to make a virtual world where the user can chat and hang out with the personality construct inside. While in this virtual world, the user's brain and thoughts run slower than usual, since processing the virtual world is run on your brain as a piece of wetware. This has the result of making time pass very quickly without the user noticing while inside the simulation.
