In the wake of a brutal cosmic disaster called the ‘rapture’, the [[C.A.T|Contamination Annihilation Team]] (C.A.T) assembled a massive labyrinthian laboratory from which it studies and builds weapons to fight against an unknown threat, for decades the Iron Heart of [[C.A.T]] stood as one of the last bastions of humanity, housing multiple city sized complexes across its entire fortress.

As experimental efforts hit a standstill, a civil war broke out when a group of sadistic scientists suggested using ‘non essential personnel’ as live test subjects for their experiments, deeming their sacrifice absolutely necessary to reclaim the surface. The conflict split the Iron Hearts territory into various factions. The extremists, deeming themselves the true C.A.T, continue their experiments with prisoners of war and the civilian populace of cities now under their control, their horrific yet awesome weaponry being made to fight the opposing complexes.
