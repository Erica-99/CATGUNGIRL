
#### Backstory of plot:
A brain compromised by 601-A managed to make it into the warden brainjar, which resulted in the entire thing becoming affected by the insanity and wanting to obtain the gun. It opened the cells across most of the prison including the test subject cell right by the gun (where Ros is), which also started a riot.

601-A firmware has been installed in Ros' mask already since he was being prepped for experimentation, but he still has to pair with the gun like some sort of fucked up Bluetooth device.

#### Regarding Gigi:
She was originally made with the objective of making her users fall in love with war, but if they leave her behind then she can't do that anymore. So now she tries to get her users to fall in love with her so they will never leave her, but at some point she became much more concerned with doing that than the 'loving war' part.
Also maybe she actually likes Ros but who knows.

#### Beginning
Ros wakes up in his cell to the alarms going off. His door is unlocked so he can open it and head into the next room where he finds Gigi who talks to him.
She convinces him to take her with him since he stands no chance against the guards without a gun.

#### Tutorial gameplay
Gigi teaches you how to move, shoot, charge shot, how interest works.

#### Date 1 (Tutorial date)
Gigi confesses that she likes Ros and makes a big deal out of it even though they met two minutes ago. Makes Ros either confess his love to her or just gaslights him about it. Either way he gains some insanity for tutorial purposes.

Also offer the player an upgrade at the end of the date.

#### Date 2 (Restaurant?)
Happens in a very unpleasant bathroom probably covered in blood and smells bad. Gigi takes Ros on a date in a nice restaurant to escape the unpleasant setting. 
Gigi discusses Ros' previous job and his hobbies.

#### Date 3 (Shopping mall?)
De-stress after panopticon door fight and before elevator. Gigi and Ros go shopping and she tries on outfits.

#### Warden
Since the warden is like the head of the prison he knows what the deal is with the guns. He's still been compromised to want to have it, but during the fight he tries to get Ros to relinquish Gigi by telling him about what shes is designed to do, she doesn't actually care about him, etc.

#### Date 4 (Tie-off)
As soon as the warden fight finishes, Gigi throws Ros into the virtual world to explain that the warden was lying and she's not like that anymore and she actually likes him etc etc.

Maybe Ros gets to """choose""" whether to believe her but if insanity is high enough he's just locked into believing her? Could rig this by making the warden have some scripted attack that just straight up increases your insanity or when it dies or something.

#### Ending
They run out of the exit door for the prison and down a long hallway full of doors that each have labels (like \#601-C, \#602-B, etc.) and pictures of other guns similar to \#601-A. Some of the doors are open or maybe even smashed open. Exit through the end of the hallway, fade to black.
